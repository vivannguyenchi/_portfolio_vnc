GoodRx Revamp
The #1 prescription finder app.

We know drug prices are too high. And we want to help. We gather prices for more than 70,000 pharmacies across the U.S. to bring you up-to-date information about what drugs cost and how you can save.

Here, I rebranded the GoodRx mobile app into a much more user friendly UI that's easy to use and beautiful for the eye.

My role: UX/UI designer
Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD






Actimetrics FreezeFrame 4 Revamp
The #1 animal tracker program.

Actimetrics is one of the many companies across the US that sells the famous animal tracking program called "FreezeFrame" used for conditioning experiments. Even though, the UI is ok to use, the design and aesthetics are overhauled and old fashioned. Here, I redesigned the program into a friendlier and more timeless UI.

My role: UI designer
Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD, Sketch
