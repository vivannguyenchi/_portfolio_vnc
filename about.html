Vivan Nguyen Chi

UX/UI Designer | Neuroscientist | Teamplayer

Hi, I am Vivan. I’m a highly organized scientist-turned UX designer, currently based in San Francisco. With more than 6+ years of working in research in an academic environment, I have honed my analytical and critical thinking skills. My passion is to discover new things in life that make the lives of others easier – and most of all, much more efficient.

I strive for perfection and therefore, with my keen eye for detail and observation, I go beyond the usual UX designer. Further, I am a strong advocate for embracing changes because that is the key factor and a driving force of progress in technology. I'm an extraverted person, so it is always a pleasure for me to interact with others and build a strong team together.

I want to further apply my skills to UX and drive the process of designing useful products and services.
